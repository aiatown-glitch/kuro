HOW IT WORKS
every KURO// artwork contains a hidden message encoded in the least significant bits (LSB) of its pixel data. the visual change is imperceptible to human eyes — less than 0.4% difference per pixel. but the data is there.
this decoder extracts those bits, reassembles them into bytes, and converts them back into text. the entire process runs locally in your browser. no data is sent anywhere. your image never leaves your device.
each KURO// garment comes with a digital original image. upload that image here to read what only AI can see.
